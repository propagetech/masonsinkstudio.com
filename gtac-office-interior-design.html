GTAC Office Interior Design

Client: Thomas Mampilly
Location: Bangalore
Area: 800sft
Status: Completed

Being a Business to Business venture the client was sure he wanted merely a workable space and was insistent on an option that was pocket friendly. A minimalistic approach was followed for the interiors of this upcoming start-up yet the space was transformed from a poorly lit, relatively plain commercial space into a bright and cheerful one. Since the space was already covered with white laminate we decided to work with it rather than complete refurbishing. We introduced splashes of lime green into the décor to bring some life into the space. The modular furniture was customized and designed such that it can be used as one large unit or several smaller ones based on need. As a result we were able to meet the pocket-friendly brief given to us making it not only “workable” but also a cheerful and bright space that people wanted to work in.
