Yash Farms

Client: Karthik Padmanabhan
Location: Yash Farm, Chandapura, Bengaluru
Area: 3400 sft
Status: Completed

Sitting on a 1.2 acre organic farm, the structure’s material composition includes Compressed Stabilized Earth Blocks (CSEB), Rammed earth and natural stone. A contemporary design inspired by the vernacular forming soulful calming spaces away from the hustle and bustle of the city. This project looks at sustainability from the micro to the macro level with rainwater harvesting, wastewater management, biogas and solar energy.
