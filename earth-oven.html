EARTH OVEN

Type: Mud Workshop - Cob technique
Location: The COurtyard, Kochi
Date: August 2017

Gaia, our personal earth oven is enjoying the spirits of nature as we type this description. The oven was designed and built during our first internal skilling workshop at Kochi. Built of cob and finished with a tadelakt waterproofing, Gaia delivers scrumptious pizzas and breads upon orders.
