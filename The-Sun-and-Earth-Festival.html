THE SUN AND EARTH FESTIVAL

Type: Earth Workshop
Location: Himalayan Institute of Alternatives, Ladakh
Date: July 2024

Our principal architect, Rosie Paul, led an engaging workshop focused on mud plastering and Moroccan lime plaster, designed to empower participants with valuable hands-on skills. Attendees explored traditional and natural plastering methods, learning how to prepare and apply both mud and lime-based plasters using sustainable, eco-friendly materials.
