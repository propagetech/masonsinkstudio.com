Casagrand Esmeralda

Client: Balamurugan & Vijayalakshmi
Location: Bengaluru
Area: 2500sft
Status: Completed

The interiors for this four-bedroom villa located in Bangalore portray a different concept and styling for each room while at the same time maintaining an overarching theme of bold colour pops that play around with hues of orange, blue and grey. The project also involved minor interventions in the civil works which enhanced the functionality of the layout. The terrace features a beautiful hand-plastered wall with Tadelakt, an ancient Morrocan technique of waterproof lime plastering.
