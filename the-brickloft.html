The Brickloft

Client: Manssi Vedhya Karambelkar
Location: Bengaluru
Area: 5500sft
Status: Completed

Going beyond the brick-and-mortar visible as a part of the building fabric, the creative processes explored give this project its speciality. From the various second-hand yards that were scavenged for doors and windows to the innovative use of crate wood as furniture – every element was carefully hand-picked. The clean lines of the glass and metal windows along with the rough finished Kota stone further complements the rustic palette of finishes that adorns this project.
